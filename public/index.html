How it works

1

Deploy the server

Run npm start or use the provided docker-compose.yml on your machine or VPS.

2

Set your domain

Point your domain's DNS A record to your server's IP. Set PUBLIC_DOMAIN and REPO_NAME in your environment.

3

Connect from iPad

Open Files → tap the … menu → Connect to Server and enter your WebDAV URL.

4

Upload & access anywhere

Browse, upload, download and organise up to 100 GB of files directly from Files or any WebDAV client.

iPad Files "Connect Server" setup

  1. Open the Files app on your iPad or iPhone.
  2. Tap the ⋯ (three dots) button in the top-right corner of the Browse tab.
  3. Tap Connect to Server.
  4. In the Server field enter your WebDAV URL:
    http://<your-domain>:3000/webdav
  5. Tap Next, choose Registered User, enter your username & password.
  6. Tap Next then Connect. Your cloud drive will appear under Locations.
